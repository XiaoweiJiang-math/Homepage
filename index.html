About me

I am a PhD student in Department of Mathematical Science, Zoom University.

Research interest: My main interest lies in birational geometry of algebraic varieties. More precisely, I am interested in topics related to Minimal Model Program (termination of flips, abundance conjecture, Iitaka conjecture) and moduli of higher dimensional varieties (boundedness, KSBA moduli theory, K-moduli theory).
