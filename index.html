About me

Im a PHD student in Department of Mathematical Science, Zoom University.

Research interest: My main interest lies in birational geometry of algebraic varieties. More precisely, I am interested in topics related to Minimal Model Program (existence of minimal models, termination of flips, nonvanishing conjecture, abundance conjecture, Iitaka conjecture) and moduli of higher dimensional varieties (boundedness, KSBA moduli theory, K-moduli theory).
